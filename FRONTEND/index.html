About GestureTalk

GestureTalk AI is designed to translate sign language gestures into readable text using computer vision and AI.

We detect hand landmarks using advanced neural networks, extract 3D keypoints, and classify them with our trained deep learning model.

Our goal is to make communication more accessible for everyone.
